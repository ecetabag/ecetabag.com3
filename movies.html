Authorial Intent on use of Multimodality

To convey my literacy story in a rich and multidimensional video series on a website, I used voice recordings, photos, and videos. This modality was designed to show my own relationship with the selected literacy in an entertaining way.

Voice recordings added dimension to my own stories, views, and insights. Images helped me visualize my literacy, important items, people, and locations. Strong visual clues capture the viewer's imagination. I tried to capture real-life situations, conversations, and places that shaped my experiences through dynamic storytelling in videos.

These approaches improve the autobiography's concepts by giving the listener a multi-sensory experience. Voice recordings express my ideas and feelings, photos provide context, and films show my literacy progress in real time. They weave a story that engages several senses and learning methods.

The viewer should approach this autobiography with an open mind and a willingness to engage with the content on multiple levels. They should actively listen, observe, and reflect on words, images, and videos. The website format allows for interactive exploration, encouraging viewers to navigate through the series at their own pace .
